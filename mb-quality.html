Quality Assurance

If there are three words that best describe our philosophy when it comes to our products these are Quality, Quality, and Quality!

Medbest SA is a trusted source for high quality, specialty food products from Greece. We work hard to ensure that the finest raw materials are used, sourcing them always from the most reliable and trusted producers. We develop solid relationships with our farmers, share our philosophy and vision with them, and make them inseparable parts of our venture. Year after year, crop after crop, we are untiringly searching for new sources of fine, “honest” ingredients, and by following the paths of tradition we are continuously working on developing new products that can be suitable additions to our line.

All our products are 100% Natural, free from preservatives, food colors and flavor enhancing agents. We monitor the usage of pesticides on our key raw materials, by providing continuous technical support to our contracted growers. In addition, our raw materials undergo extensive testing by accredited laboratories for pesticide residues, to ensure that our products are free from pesticides and chemicals that unfortunately are common in most agricultural products these days.

All our products are manufactured under the strictest quality assurance and good manufacturing practices (GMP). For our professional QC team quality control means hands-on involvement in every step of the production process, ensuring the quality of the final product. Our manufacturing facilities besides HACCP and ISO 22000, they are certified under B.R.C., and I.F.S. which are top tier accreditations in the modern food manufacturing. Additionally, our facilities have F.C.E. registration numbers with FDA (Food & Drug Administration) which licenses them to sell to the United States of America.

Our traceability systems ensure that the raw material in each unit produced can be traced back to its origin, which brings to life our vision to have together with our business partners abroad absolute control of our products “from the tree to the table”.
