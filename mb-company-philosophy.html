Philosophy & vision

Medbest SA was established in 1996 with the objective to create and offer a complete line of authentic Greek Specialty Food products to the sophisticated international consumer that seeks high quality, originality, innovation, value and exquisite flavors.

Today, Medbest SA is among the leading Greek export oriented manufacturing Companies, and our products can be found in some of the highest quality retail food stores, and fine dining restaurants, all over the world. We are 100% export Company and our products are successfully sold in over 20 countries worldwide, among which are the United States of America, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Japan, South America, South Africa and others. Our Company Strategy is to build strong business relationships and strategic alliances with reliable partners internationally, that import and distribute our products to their markets. We have the experience the knowledge and the determination to support them effectively, in order to build a long mutually beneficial cooperation.

The philosophy behind our successful enterprise has been the same since the day we started. We value and honor our relationships with our partners, whether these are the growers of our olives in some small village in Greece, our co-packers, or a strategic customer in a foreign country far from home. To us, the most important thing is to hold the chain “from the tree to the table” intact, by preserving the relationships through honesty and integrity.

We pride ourselves on supporting the farmers and the local communities that produce the raw materials for our products. These communities rely on us and our work to stay and make a living in their home land, raise their families and work the land their ancestors did for centuries, instead of moving into the large urban centers, deserting their homes, their way of life and their traditions. These are the same traditions that allow generations to continue the craft perfected by their ancestors.

We support Organic Farming, and have included several Organic products in our line. Our Company is certified by D.I.O. for the E.U. and by USDA for the American N.O.P.
